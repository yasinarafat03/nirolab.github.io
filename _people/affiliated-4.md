Dr. Fariah Mahzabeen is an Associate Professor and Undergraduate Coordinator (EEE/ETE) in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at North South University. She previously held a tenure-track position at San Jose State University and brings industry experience from Google, Verily, and Meta in Silicon Valley.

## Education

- **PhD** in Electrical Engineering, Stanford University, USA
- **MS** in Electrical Engineering, Stanford University, USA
- **Postdoctoral Training** in Electrical Engineering, Stanford University, USA

## Research Focus

Dr. Mahzabeen's research focuses on interdisciplinary ideas that combine hardware and software across domains including biosensors, wearables, IoT, AI, and machine learning, with emphasis on developing solutions for health, education, and environmental challenges.
